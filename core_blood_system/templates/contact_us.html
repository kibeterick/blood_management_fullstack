Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I can't register for an account. What should I do?
A: If you're experiencing registration issues, please contact us via phone (+254 700 123 456) or email (support@bloodmanagement.com). Provide your full name, email address, and a description of the error you're encountering. Our team will create an account for you or resolve the issue within 24 hours.
Q: I submitted a blood request but haven't received any feedback. What's happening?
A: Blood requests are typically reviewed within 24-48 hours. If you haven't received feedback after this period, please contact us immediately with your request details. We'll check the status and provide an update. For urgent requests, call our emergency hotline: +254 700 123 456.
Q: How long does it take to get a response?
A: Phone calls are answered immediately during business hours. Emails are typically responded to within 24 hours. WhatsApp messages receive responses within a few hours. For emergencies, call our 24/7 hotline.
Q: Can I track my blood request status?
A: Yes! Once logged in, go to your dashboard to view all your blood requests and their current status (Pending, Approved, Fulfilled, or Cancelled). If you need more details, contact us.
Q: What information should I provide when contacting support?
A: Please provide: Your full name, email address or phone number, a detailed description of your issue, and any relevant screenshots or error messages. This helps us resolve your issue faster.
